Business Insights and Recommendations Based on Sales Analysis

Project Overview:
This project analyzes business performance to uncover key insights and provide actionable recommendations. By leveraging Power BI, the analysis explores critical trends in shipping and sales, regional and category performance, and customer behavior. The goal is to identify areas for improvement and deliver strategies to enhance operational efficiency and profitability.

Key Insights

  • Shipping and Sales Trends: The Standard Class shipping mode dominates with 850.94% more sales than Same Day. Sales rose significantly, with a 3,921.62% increase between January 1, 2019, and December 31, 2020, and a sharp surge (157.10%) from August 13, 2020, to September 22, 2020.
  • Profit Insights: Technology and Office Supplies categories lead in profitability, but profit margins declined by 66.66% between January 2019 and December 2020. West and East regions show higher profits, with California and New York as top performers.
  • Customer and Segment Patterns: The Consumer Segment contributes the most profit, while the Corporate Segment has the highest average delivery time (4.01 days).
  • Regional Insights: California and Texas lead in sales and total costs. COD (Cash on Delivery) and Office Supplies perform better in the West and East regions.
  • Outliers and Clusters: Clusters between Profit Percentage and Delivery Time were observed, along with anomalies on specific dates like September 8, 2019, and December 2, 2019.
  • Category-Level Insights: Technology dominates profits, while the Paper sub-category shows high margins. Tables underperform consistently.

Recommendations

  • Optimize Standard Class: Address high logistics costs for Standard Class to maintain profitability.
  • Boost Underperforming Sub-Categories: Improve pricing and promotions for Tables while focusing on high-margin sub-categories like Phones and Accessories.
  • Expand COD and Online Payments: Leverage CODâ€™s popularity and incentivize online payments to minimize risks.
  • Address Declining Profit Margins: Investigate factors behind the profit margin drop and renegotiate supplier contracts.
  • Leverage Regional Strengths: Invest in high-performing regions like California and New York while expanding COD and Office Supplies in the West and East.
  • Improve Delivery Efficiency: Use clustering insights to streamline shipping schedules and address delivery anomalies.
  • Plan for Seasonality: Use historical trends to prepare for peak months like December and strategize inventory and staffing.

Proposed Actions

  • Implement cost-saving measures in Standard Class logistics.
  • Boost marketing for high-profit categories and underperforming sub-categories.
  • Expand COD and Online Payments availability.
  • Investigate delivery issues on specific outlier dates.
  • Focus on maintaining high-performing regions and expanding market reach.
  • Monitor and address declining profit margins with operational adjustments.
